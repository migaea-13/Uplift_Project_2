About the app

The Submeter Electricity Calculator is a web application designed to fairly distribute electricity costs among multiple tenants using submeters. The app calculates individual electricity consumption based on submeter readings and generates a bill breakdown accordingly. It supports two billing scenarios:

  • Individual Billing:

    Each submeter pays for their exact usage.

  • Shared Billing:

    Excess charges from the primary meter are equally distributed.
